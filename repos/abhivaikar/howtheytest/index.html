How They Test

Have you ever wonder which path has led some of the major players to their current quality culture? On https://abhivaikar.github.io/howtheytest/, you can find all of the blog posts, articles and other resources that describe just that. Usually you would have to scour the internet to try and get a coherent picture, but no more! How They Test has gathered these resources for you and grouped them together for companies like Airbnb, Amazon, Apple, and Atlassian. And that’s just the “A”!

As not to forget to come back to those resources, this repo got starred!
